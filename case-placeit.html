Case Study: Placeit by Envato

Scaling internal tools for content operations

Context

Placeit is a SaaS platform offering realistic mockups, customizable templates, and stock assets primarily for print-on-demand businesses. The company’s strength lies in its large, high-quality content library, produced in-house by a specialized team of designers and photographers.

As a Product Manager, I focused on the internal platform used by the content team to upload and manage these assets, ensuring a smooth pipeline between content creation and the customer-facing site.
